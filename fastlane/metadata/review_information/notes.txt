CALarm requires iOS 26 or later for AlarmKit.

No login or account is required. Calendar data stays on the device; nothing is uploaded to our servers.

To test:
1. Grant Calendar access when prompted.
2. Grant Alarms permission when prompted (Settings → CALarm if denied).
3. Open the week schedule, tap an event, and add one or more alarm offsets.
4. Observe the countdown in a Live Activity on the Lock Screen or Dynamic Island.

There is no demo account. The app works entirely with the reviewer’s on-device calendar.
