CALarm Support

CALarm is a calendar-aware countdown alarm app for iOS 26+ using Apple’s AlarmKit, Live Activities, and EventKit.

Contact

Email: parth.chandak02@gmail.com

Requirements

  • iOS 26 or later
  • Calendar permission (read upcoming events)
  • Alarms permission (AlarmKit)

Common issues

No events showing. Open Settings → Privacy & Security → Calendars and allow CALarm. Return to the app and tap Refresh.

Alarms not firing. Open Settings → CALarm and ensure alarms are allowed. Confirm the event alarm is enabled in CALarm.

Live Activity not visible. Ensure Live Activities are enabled for CALarm in iOS Settings.
